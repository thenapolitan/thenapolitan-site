Design Review

DFM Review

Manufacturability problems caught before fab are free. Caught during production, they're expensive. Caught in the field, they're catastrophic. A thorough DFM review is the best insurance you can buy.

Gerber, ODB++, and board layout review with a focus on real-world SMT process constraints — not just automated rule-check output.

Submit Files for Review →
  • Gerber and ODB++ file review for land pattern, courtyard, and clearance issues
  • Stencil aperture analysis — area ratio, paste release, and bridging risk assessment
  • Component placement review for solderability, rework access, and thermal shadowing
  • X-ray and AXI inspection planning — identifying areas requiring cross-sectional or 3D inspection coverage
  • Conformal coating and underfill compatibility review
  • Via-in-pad, BGA escape routing, and buried/blind via assessment
  • Board panelization and depaneling review for warpage and stress management
