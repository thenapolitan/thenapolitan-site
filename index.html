I'm Chris Villareale — an independent consultant based in Southern California with over a decade of hands-on experience in avionics and defense PCBA manufacturing. Every engagement is handled personally by me. No subcontractors, no junior staff.

My background spans the full SMT process: pick-and-place programming, stencil printing, reflow profiling, X-ray and AXI inspection, conformal coating, underfill, and IPC-A-610 acceptance criteria. I've written SOPs, set up lines, and managed processes for Class 2 and Class 3 assemblies.

I combine that manufacturing depth with a growing web design practice — building real applications that actually ship, deployed on modern infrastructure.
